Overview

This platform distills complex household energy records into intuitive, high-impact visual analytics. By identifying and highlighting peak efficiency patterns, we provide the intelligence necessary for meaningful sustainability analysis.

Intelligence Layer

The dashboard leverages a refined dataset of synchronous electricity records. Our focus is anchored on Low Active Power cycles—the definitive signature of energy efficiency in the modern home—to drive towards a lower carbon footprint.
